## Proof question

Can a 2–4 player team experience the locked Expedition / session-structured living reactive gas sandbox as a coherent expedition judgment loop — choose risk → enter → read gas/topology/value → alter route or flow → create temporary safety or access → recover and secure unstable value → manage readable gas/cargo consequence → push, reroute, secure, retreat or abandon — rather than as an abstract gas simulation or loose gas prototype?

## Link to locked bet

This proof question is tied to the selected primary bet:

Expedition / session-structured living reactive gas sandbox

It tests the locked experience model at shape level:

A bounded co-op expedition run where players enter an unknown living gas/topology/cargo environment, create temporary access or safety, handle unstable value, and make push / reroute / retreat / abandon decisions under readable environmental consequence.

The question is not asking whether gas simulation is technically impressive.

It is asking whether the gas/topology/cargo system produces the selected Expedition experience.

## Следующий Goal проверяет

Следующий Goal проверяет, работает ли выбранная Expedition bet as-played at minimum contour level:

- the team understands the situation as an expedition, not as crisis containment or freeform sandbox testing;
- gas and topology create readable route pressure;
- temporary safety or access can be created through spatial decisions;
- unstable value creates extraction commitment and cargo risk;
- gas/cargo consequences are readable enough to support agency;
- co-op matters because players share one route, gas and cargo state;
- the meaningful run decision becomes push, reroute, secure, retreat or abandon;
- the proof result can honestly support a go / revise / kill judgment on this selected bet.
