# Play: local/marketing-forge

Purpose: forge ONE marketing question — a DECISION or an ARTIFACT — from open to frozen, in the solo-dev's authentic voice. The owner has NO marketing/social expertise, so each card is decided by fresh RESEARCH + his own inputs, never a generic playbook; he supplies voice, taste, the final yes. The plan, goals, and channels are NOT pre-set — they are DERIVED inside, one card at a time. Marketing is CO-CREATED. Runs PARALLEL to the build bet — never a second active bet (G1), never touches NOW.md.

Reads: work/marketing/board.md + the chosen card + frozen parents; work/marketing-track-design-2026-06-16.md (how the track works); TREE.md (g-2f8c); CHARTER.md (lenses, AI-imagery policy).

Writes: the LEDGER under work/marketing/ — the frozen card (the decision/artifact + its research+inputs basis + owner voice-verdict), its rejected_alternatives, child cards, board.md. OS state: LOG.md (one line). PROPOSES knowledge/ facts (read_by:). Never NOW.md or another track's repo.

Precondition: a card chosen from local/marketing-status's actionable set (depends_on frozen, no open cross-track request). One chat = one card.

## Steps
1. **Frame** — restate the question; name its TYPE (DECISION / ARTIFACT); inherit frozen parents it must not contradict (cite ids).
2. **Research + the owner's inputs** (the gate that stands in for owner expertise) — research THIS question for OUR situation (solo, niche, introverted) + gather the owner's context; output 2–4 options, each with its bet + an HONEST basis (a real case or fact). Numbers are DERIVED here with basis, never invented or owner-guessed.
3. **Cross-track check** — needs the world/hook (→ canon) or a real build moment (→ dev)? If yes: record the request on the card (`cross_track {track, status: open}` + what / why), mark BLOCKED, stop. Never invent lore, fake a build moment, or write another track's repo / NOW. A request to DEV only RECOMMENDS / surfaces what the active bet already guarantees — never adds scope (G1/G2); the owner relays it.
4. **Choose (owner)** — options in plain language; the owner picks / mixes / steers in his words. `(owner)`
5. **Draft** — write the card: DECISION = the choice + why + what it unblocks; ARTIFACT = what to make / where / the message + a sample in the owner's voice. Mark DELEGABLE logistics vs OWNER-ONLY voice.
6. **Voice gate (owner closes)** — AI checks for AI-tells + strikes any concrete not traceable to what the owner said or did; robotic-but-structurally-fine → humanize (AI "make it human" pass, rework, or rewrite). The OWNER reads last: живой / фейк — only his pass freezes. Public media stays human-made. Fail → step 5. `(owner)`
7. **Freeze & grow (owner)** — owner signs → status: frozen; dropped options → rejected_alternatives; spawn the child cards this answer forces (depends_on); update board.md. `(owner)`

## Done when
One frozen card in work/marketing/; its research+inputs basis + owner voice-verdict recorded; cross-track requests recorded where needed; child cards spawned; board.md + one LOG line; owner's sign recorded. NOW.md untouched; no NOW bet (G1).

## Notes
- Co-creation is binding: steps 4, 6, 7 are `(owner)`; voice never freezes without his verdict and is never AI-generated for public channels.
- Cadence is uneven BY DESIGN: take a card when there's time, close it, leave the rest — gated on "something real to say," not a treadmill.
- The PLAN is an OUTPUT of this process, never baked up front: the path, goals, and channels are derived card by card from research + the owner's inputs.
- Delegation is an optional valve, not a bet: default = owner runs the card; helpers take logistics only, if overloaded.
