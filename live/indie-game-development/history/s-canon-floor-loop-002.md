     # Петля этажа/слоя: Simple Gas Work Loop

     ## Ответ (канон)

     **Инварианты — на это сверяется future floor verbs, floor intel, field legibility, opportunity grammar, cargo/containment, return liability, co-op roles and implementation:**

     1. **Floor loop — это Simple Gas Work Loop поверх Simple Floor Read.**
        Команда входит на floor/cluster с грубым, но реальным read, превращает его в рабочее понимание через поле,
        работает с gas value and active conditions, создаёт или отклоняет stake, получает return liability, временно
        распределяет роли и решает: продолжать добывать, push'ить, стабилизировать, идти глубже, возвращаться, отступать
        или бросать value.

        Short form:

        `enter with floor read → field contact → gas/value work → stake/liability changes → greed/return/deeper decision → repeat or handoff`.

        Это не "зачистить комнату", не "поднять один loot", не "решить puzzle" and not "vacuum all gas".

     2. **Floor loop starts with real floor read, not fake uncertainty.**
        Pre-entry read может быть грубым, неполным и не объяснять всё. Но если read сообщает active condition, этот
        condition **есть**.

        Forbidden form:

        "Возможно, тут есть radiation leak, возможно, нет."

        Correct form:

        "Тут есть radiation leak signal. До входа не ясно: где источник, какой масштаб, какой точный тип, насколько он
        влияет на gas state, можно ли его использовать, можно ли его обойти, и есть ли рядом ещё скрытые условия."

        Туман держится не на "condition существует или нет", а на деталях:
        - точный источник;
        - масштаб;
        - тип;
        - доступность;
        - связь с газом;
        - влияние на route/cargo/players;
        - скрытые дополнительные условия;
        - безопасен ли он для работы or only for avoidance.

        Scanner/intel later may reveal more of these details, but floor loop must not become a lottery about whether the
        advertised condition exists.

     3. **Field confirmation is progressive, not a fast doorway check.**
        Field confirmation не означает "вошли и сразу всё подтвердили".

        Команда может уточнять floor read по-разному:
        - сразу у входа видно, что leak идёт из трубы;
        - источник radiation в дальнем крыле, и до него надо добраться;
        - cold pocket найден позже и меняет план;
        - route сначала кажется безопасным, но портится после push;
        - команда понимает только часть mechanics condition and still makes a working decision.

        Canon formula:

        `Field confirmation turns coarse read into usable operational knowledge through movement, observation, bounded actions and consequences.`

        Players do not need to solve the floor at the door. They enter with a read, start working, and discover what is
        actually possible by contacting the floor.

     4. **Environment is not re-confirmed as a checklist.**
        If pre-entry read already says "cold storage" or "hot compressor area", players do not need a ritual phase called
        "confirm environment".

        In the field, the important question is what this local environment does now:
        - cold preserves a sample or only slows a reaction;
        - hot raises yield or breaks containment too quickly;
        - pressure-heavy area helps push or makes return worse;
        - cold pocket is usable as a tool or sits behind bad route;
        - contaminated area affects cargo, air, route or all of them.

        Not "confirm the environment".
        Correct feel: "learn what this local environment does to gas/value/route/cargo in this floor."

     5. **Active condition is world-cause/context, not loot by default.**
        Active condition не равен "предмет, который надо вынести".

        It can be:
        - source: radiation leak, pressure source, hot leak, cold sink;
        - area: contaminated bypass, draft shaft, cold pocket, hot corridor;
        - machine/state: broken scrubber, compressor, sealed chamber, damaged containment;
        - material: organics, blood, catalyst dust, biomaterial;
        - route factor: a path that is safe/dangerous only under a certain gas state;
        - gas modifier in-world: physical cause that changes gas behavior.

        Sometimes a condition can become loot or extractable value. That is one possibility, not the default.

        Example:
        Radiation source stands in a room. The team does not carry it home as reward. It changes nearby gas states:
        samples close to it may become more valuable but less stable; cargo may degrade faster; route may become more
        dangerous; shared air may get worse. The team decides whether to work near it, route around it, use it for a push,
        stabilize the result, or leave.

        This is allowed as a "modifier" only in the physical/world-cause sense. It is not a numeric affix like `+15% rare gas`.

     6. **The center of floor loop is gas/value work.**
        Команда входит на floor не ради одного pickup. Она хочет добыть или создать как можно больше **полезной газовой
        ценности**, while that remains rational.

        Early value may be any useful readable gas/sample/procedure. Later value should trend toward better forms:
        - clean sample;
        - stable state-window;
        - pushed regime output;
        - route proof;
        - tool charge;
        - readiness material;
        - rare/deep/volatile state;
        - knowledge/procedure that improves future runs.

        But "more" does not mean raw-volume vacuuming. Parent canon already locks value into state-window, regime, purity,
        stability, push/stabilize potential and safe return.

        Broad floor intents:
        - **capture** — take a bounded gas/sample/state-window;
        - **push** — make a state more valuable through environment/condition;
        - **stabilize** — make a state more returnable for a cost;
        - **route** — open, worsen, avoid, clean, use or lose a path;
        - **condition use/avoid** — work with or around an active condition without necessarily extracting it;
        - **release/quench/abandon** — dump or degrade value to protect survival/route/cargo control;
        - **learn/mark** — get knowledge, procedure, route confidence or readiness signal even without major cargo.

        This is not an exact verb list. Exact tools, controls, UI, inventory, room verbs, capacity, balance and scanner
        model remain downstream.

     7. **A meaningful floor action must change stake.**
        Future concrete mechanics must pass this test:

        `After the action, what changed in the world or expedition stake?`

        Valid changes:
        - cargo state became better, worse, more dangerous, more stable or more returnable;
        - gas state became readable, captured, pushed, spoiled, released;
        - route became safer, worse, contaminated, blocked, opened or time-sensitive;
        - active condition became understood, used, avoided, isolated or made the situation worse;
        - player temporarily occupied a role;
        - shared air/player safety changed;
        - team gained knowledge/procedure/readiness;
        - return liability grew or was reduced for a cost;
        - deeper/return/retreat decision changed.

        Invalid form:

        "UI says +10% quality, but nothing happened in the field."

     8. **Greed pressure repeats inside the floor.**
        Floor loop is not necessarily:

        `found value → took it → left`.

        Better shape:

        `found value → captured/pushed/stabilized → saw another possibility → risk changed → continued or returned`.

        Team constantly compares:
        - take more;
        - take better;
        - push current state;
        - stabilize and lose ceiling;
        - carry deeper;
        - return now;
        - release value to save route/player;
        - mark the floor as "later with better prep".

        This is local greed/fear. It is not a popup, not a UI window, and not a forced pause. The situation makes players ask:
        "Are we still greedy, or is it time to leave?"

     9. **Commit window is situational only, not UI/menu.**
        If the term is used later, it means only this:

        `Commit window is a readable situation where the team understands that the next action changes the expedition stake.`

        Examples:
        - cargo is already valuable but unstable;
        - route back worsened;
        - next gas pocket looks richer;
        - carrier is occupied and rescue flexibility is lower;
        - stabilization lowers risk but kills high-value push potential;
        - deeper path is open, but current stake is not safe;
        - release saves people but destroys value.

        Forbidden form:

        "The game opens a bank / push / continue menu."

        Correct form:

        "Players argue: take more or leave?"

     10. **Temporary co-op roles emerge from stake, not classes.**
         Floor loop must not force every cheap sample into a full-team operation.

         But meaningful/progression/valuable/unstable gas work should create temporary role pressure. Roles are situational,
         instrumental, and may be swapped. They are not permanent RPG classes and not required named UI roles.

         Possible temporary roles:
         - **reader/caller** — reads gas/front/condition/route cues and calls changes;
         - **carrier/custody** — carries or maintains captured/pushed state-window;
         - **condition holder/stabilizer** — holds cold/hot/pressure/isolation/quench/control context;
         - **route/anchor** — preserves path, bypass, door, lift/shaft relation, return lane;
         - **rescue/release** — prepares to release, quench, abandon, carry a player, or stabilize worse.

         Cheap/simple/early samples may be solo-handled. Meaningful descent/progression value should strain team coverage.

     11. **Return liability is local, state-based, and not a universal timer.**
         Floor creates return liability when gas/value work changes the world.

         Liability may come from:
         - leaking/spoiling/unstable cargo;
         - route contamination;
         - worse shared air;
         - hot/pressure path on return;
         - spent stabilizer/tool/route option;
         - carrier occupied;
         - downed/injured/slow player;
         - valuable cargo that should not be carried deeper;
         - active condition worsened after push;
         - raw bulk that became bad cargo.

         This continues `q-expedition-macrocycle`: pressure should come from readable return debt, not a default global
         "20 minutes until wipe" rule.

     12. **Base prepares/processes; field creates the living stake.**
         Base can prepare the team, process returned value, improve readiness, store procedures, stabilize returned output,
         and help future runs.

         But base must not cheaply create the same value that the field creates through:
         local environment + active conditions + found state-window + capture/push risk + containment + return.

         If base can cheaply make the same result, floor loop collapses into base crafting.

     13. **Floor loop hands state to expedition macrocycle.**
         Floor does not end only with "success/fail".

         Clean handoff states:
         - **continue inside floor** — team keeps working this cluster;
         - **go deeper** — team carries readiness/stake deeper;
         - **return/bank attempt** — team decides to bring value to safe return;
         - **retreat** — team leaves with little/no value but preserves people/future readiness;
         - **release/abandon** — value is dumped to protect survival/route;
         - **partial disaster** — cargo/route/player state damaged, but not necessarily full failure;
         - **failure seed** — safe return failed or later recovery/failure model is invoked;
         - **knowledge/readiness handoff** — team gained procedure/route confidence/understanding even if cargo is small.

     14. **Paper canon does not certify fun.**
         This card freezes the design direction and passes paper gates. It does not prove pacing, execution, onboarding,
         readability under real noise, or actual two-player fun. Those require later grey-box evidence with real humans.

     ### Проза — простыми словами

     Команда не заходит на floor как в "уровень с лутом".

     Она примерно знает: куда идёт, какая там среда, какие active conditions там есть или какие сигналы уже видны, и зачем
     это может быть полезно. Но она не знает всех деталей: где источник, как сильно он влияет на gas, можно ли использовать
     его безопасно, какие дополнительные условия скрыты, насколько хорош route назад.

     Внутри команда начинает работать. Она видит gas pockets, утечки, cold/hot/pressure zones, органику, radiation source,
     broken machinery, contaminated path. Active condition может быть не loot. Оно может просто сидеть в мире и менять gas
     behavior. Игроки решают: взять sample, сделать её дороже рядом с condition, стабилизировать хуже, обойти condition,
     использовать route, сбросить value или уйти.

     Цель — вынести как можно больше полезной gas value, но не через "пылесосим всё". Ценность в state: clean sample,
     stable regime, pushed output, rare/deep state, route proof, procedure, readiness. Чем жаднее команда работает, тем
     больше она создаёт долг перед return: груз течёт, путь хуже, воздух хуже, один игрок занят, rescue margin меньше.

     Хороший floor creates a living argument:

     "Берём ещё или уходим?"
     "Пушим этот state или стабилизируем дешевле?"
     "Этот radiation source делает sample дороже, но route назад станет хуже."
     "Carrier занят, если сейчас полезем глубже — спасать будет некому."
     "Бросаем sample и выводим человека."
     "Мы не взяли много cargo, но поняли, как этот condition работает — в следующий заход вернёмся подготовленнее."

     ### Example

     Floor read before entry:

     "Cold medical storage. Есть organics. Есть radiation leak signal. Среда холодная. Точная форма radiation source неизвестна.
     Могут быть дополнительные скрытые факторы."

     Inside, the team does not quickly confirm everything. They move through storage and see stable cold pockets. Later it
     becomes clear: near radiation source, some gas samples gain a more valuable state but become less stable.

     Radiation source is not loot. The team does not carry it home. It is part of the place and an active condition.
     It can be used as risky push context or avoided.

     The team can:
     - take safe cold sample and leave;
     - move another sample closer to radiation source and push value;
     - use cold pocket to stabilize pushed result;
     - avoid contaminated route;
     - abandon unstable sample if route becomes bad;
     - return later with better prep/intel.

     If the team pushes, value becomes better, but carrier is now occupied, cargo holds worse, and route through contaminated
     section is riskier. Temporary roles appear: one carries/holds state, another watches route/cold pocket, another prepares
     release/rescue.

     There is no UI "commit window." There is a living question:

     "We already made good value. Take another pocket, stabilize and leave, go deeper with this cargo, or drop part of it for safe return?"

     # Floor gas work verbs: как игрок реально делает capture / push / stabilize / route / release?

     ## Вопрос

     Какие exact player-facing verbs and controls реализуют Simple Gas Work Loop внутри floor: capture, push, stabilize,
     route, condition use/avoid, release/quench/abandon and learn/mark — без большого fiddly action set, без wiki/spreadsheet,
     без преждевременного scanner/tool/loadout lock and without collapsing into vacuum-cleaner play?

     ## Почему вопрос появился
