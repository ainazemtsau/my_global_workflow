Key findings folded (representative, not exhaustive — full detail in the two workflow outputs and
work/gas-visual-plan-v2-2026-07-02.md):
- Lineup mode / two-gas-meet cannot render in Stage 1 (real feed is single-species, `G=0` hardcoded) — moved
  to Stage 4, after the W1b read-API lands.
- A golden-replay CLIP LIBRARY was rejected in favor of a fixed-seed restart + serialized emitter config: the
  sim is already deterministic, and a blessed-clip library would rot every time Sc-reactions/Sc-typing change
  sim behavior mid-track.
- Deferring real half-res to a late perf stage would retroactively invalidate every full-res owner-eye verdict
  given earlier (GTX1060 evidence: 7–11 ms full-res vs ~1 ms half-res; upsampling visibly alters thin rims/
  dither/erosion) — pulled forward to Stage 2.
- The schema/layout-ADR decision must precede hero-polish (Stage 3), else polish gets re-tuned when the 96→
  128-byte layout lands — a manufactured regression for an owner already burned twice by "it got worse".
- W1b (the per-cell dominant-type read-API) is a `Core/Field` edit and cannot ride inside a render-only leg —
  re-scoped out of the visual track entirely into its own tiny engine mini-CALL.
- The lineup/naming acceptance test needs blinding (pods shuffled, owner names cold) — a familiar reviewer
  naming his own co-created archetypes proves nothing about a naive player's glance-read.
- Danger's hue-toward-heat shift can compress the very hue axis Stage 3's type-separation was signed on —
  Stage 4 acceptance now re-runs the blind lineup at danger 0/0.5/1.0.
- The stand must include an open-space no-walls bookmark (owner correction: gas spawns anywhere, directional
  jets in the open are a felt requirement — not room-only), per [[feedback-no-test-rigs-for-head-decisions]].
- Film look-dev calibration props (grey/chrome spheres, Macbeth chart, 3 lighting presets) pay no rent for a
  raymarched emissive volume with no surface BRDF — trimmed from the minimal stand.
