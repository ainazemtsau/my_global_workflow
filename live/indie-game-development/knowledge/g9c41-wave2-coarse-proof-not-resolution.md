# Transport+consistency proven ≠ the game feels resolved — the coarse render has a legibility ceiling

read_by: review/shape (do NOT pin "owner satisfaction" or "feels like the game" to a seam/transport proof); the core-gameplay-depth lens (g-d3a8).

Accepted 2026-06-20 by review s-review-002 (forecast-surprise of the Wave-2 close).

**Fact.** Wave 2 proved exactly what it forecast — the coarse band solver is numerically stable, integer-exact-replicable host+2c, runs on a real DA level, and renders legibly (heavy-low red / light-high blue, per-room stratification). The forecast risks (float→integer reduction, coarse replication, in-budget convergence) all HELD. And yet the bet's output produces ZERO gameplay decisions (code-verified: CoarseFillProjection is a read-only render projection; the only other read-model consumer, ExposureQuery, is a seam with NO damage/exposure law behind it). The owner, SEEING the honest coarse render, signed the legibility floor "confirmed с оговоркой" — "базовое работает и наблюдаемо… не всё понятно — возможно ограничение структуры" — and immediately raised d-gasmodel-redesign-001.

So the surprise was NOT in the forecast's risk axis (wrong-mechanism for "satisfaction") + an optimistic "player-facing terminus = showable artifact" (it was re-scoped to a non-showable internal proof). The lesson: **a seam/transport proof can be fully MET and still not move "does this feel like the game I want"**, because depth and "where exactly is the gas" come from what the MODEL can RESOLVE (a real front, intra-room place, resident-gas consequence) — not from "more layers consistent" or a prettier render. Seeing an honest coarse render is precisely what GENERATED the model-redesign requirement; coarse-only legibility has a real ceiling.

**How to apply.** When shaping or reviewing a bet whose deliverable is a seam/transport/consistency proof, do NOT word its success as "the owner will be satisfied" or "this is the game" — word it as "this proves the seam/transport, explicitly NOT depth/feel," and EXPECT a requirement shift once the owner sees it honestly. Put the cheap honest proof FIRST anyway (it correctly informed the redesign — the coarse proof is what told us we need the fine tier / front / resident-gas law), but budget for the follow-on the proof will reveal. Depth-from-the-model (front + resident-gas damage law) now converges with g-d3a8's "every gas carries a gameplay job."
