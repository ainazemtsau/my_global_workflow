# Mechanics Workbench — Question Queue v0

Status: queue draft, не canon.
Purpose: держать порядок вопросов, чтобы владелец не искал его по файлам.

## Current rule

Only one question is active per session.

If a side topic appears, it is captured and parked, not solved in the same session.

Route:
Design Lab clears unclear blockers. When the Bubble loop becomes concrete enough, it moves to `local/mechanic-forge`.
Only after a worked playable loop exists should `local/canon-forge` freeze compact canon cards.

## Q1 — What does "gas is here" mean in the first proof?

Owner-facing question:

> В первом proof, когда мы говорим "здесь есть газ", что именно существует в пространстве: слой, объем, поток, источник, видимое озеро, почти невидимая концентрация со следами, или другое?

Why now:
without this, "перевести газ в пузырь" is vague.

Blocks:

- detection/read;
- transfer-to-bubble;
- bubble size/behavior;
- failure/release;
- reactions;
- co-op route work.

Not discussing:
exact tools, exact membrane, economy, `ЛЁГКИЕ`, full gas roster, final names, final VFX, title.

Expected result:
choose one first-proof spatial model, or split the question if it is too broad.

## Q2 — What do players read before touching the gas?

Why after Q1:
read depends on what exists spatially.

Blocks:
fairness, detection, instrument role, first 60 seconds, tutorial/no-tutorial.

## Q3 — What is the transfer-to-bubble action?

Why after Q1/Q2:
we need to know what gas form exists and how players identify it before converting any part to bubble.

Blocks:
tool placeholder, player verbs, co-op action, risk during capture.

## Q4 — How is the bubble carried?

Why after Q3:
carry depends on bubble creation, size and relation to gas contents.

Blocks:
co-op body mechanics, route layout, failure triggers.

## Q5 — What happens on rupture / leak / release?

Why after Q4:
failure must be attributable and world-changing.

Blocks:
reactions, recovery, return liability, one-more pull.

## Q6 — Why do two players matter in the moment?

Why after Q1-Q5:
anti-solo proof needs the actual read/transfer/carry/failure shape.

Blocks:
greybox criteria.

## Q7 — Does the bare floor loop create "one more"?

Why after Q1-Q6:
meta must amplify replay pull, not invent it.

Blocks:
`ЛЁГКИЕ`, base, economy, run structure.
