## 2. Reconciliation verdict

The former ready front P0 → Q1 is no longer current.

Former Q1 and Q4 now have accepted non-canon Frame-level answers:
- the Sphere actively captures nearby gameplay gas;
- gas moves visibly into a growing Sphere;
- the player chooses position, amount and stopping point;
- after closing, the Sphere remains free physical cargo;
- short straps provide sustained control;
- bodily impulses, route and occupied volume remain relevant;
- shell, gas and volume remain separate physical causes;
- return uses an elevator or equivalent bounded physical space.

Universal capture is also settled at non-canon Frame altitude:
- every standard Sphere captures and holds every gameplay gas;
- gas type alone does not damage, weaken, destabilize or rupture it;
- no mandatory special Sphere category or gas × shell matrix exists;
- possible mixed-gas consequences belong only to future common
  gas × gas rules;
- gas-specific specialization/instability is PARKED behind playable
  evidence.

These conclusions are parent locks for routing. They are not canon.
