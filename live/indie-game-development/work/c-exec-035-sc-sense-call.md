## Why this slice

The sim has authoritative gas everywhere, but NOTHING reads it AS A PLAYER: there is no actor entity, no player-position input, no per-player exposure, no dose. The one exposure primitive (`ExposureQuery`) sits on the 2-band tier that S4 deletes and has NO runtime consumer. Damage, breathing («Лёгкие»), detection, and co-op rescue ALL key off «how much of which gas is a player in, over time» — and none of it can exist until that read exists on the authoritative near field, deterministically, with the player position as a lockstep input. Sc-sense builds exactly that foundation — canon-independent, player-facing, the front of Sc-damage.

## goal (outcome, not method)

Given one or more player-ACTORS whose position/volume is a **lockstep input**, the sim computes, deterministically on every peer, **per-actor per-gas-type EXPOSURE from the AUTHORITATIVE NEAR field** and **integrates it into a DOSE over time** (time-accumulated), and exposes a **readable output** — «actor A is in Gas-T at instantaneous exposure E / accumulated dose D» — that a later consequence (Sc-damage), a breathing meta, or a detection mechanic can key off. The owner SEES, in a Unity debug scene, a **capsule** he moves through a gas cloud with a live readout: inside gas → dose climbs; out of gas → dose stops; different gas → different readout. **ENGINE-ONLY (the dose read + integration live in headless Core/**; the capsule + readout are the Unity owner-eye view) — NO damage/consequence, NO detection-gameplay mechanic, NO visual pipeline, NO new gas types.**

**approach token = `authoritative-near-field-per-actor-dose-deterministic-lockstep`** — the ONE substrate: per-actor exposure read from the NEAR sparse-dominant authoritative field (NOT the doomed 2-band), actor position as a lockstep input, integer time-integrated dose that is byte-identical on every peer. A delivered artifact whose recorded `approach:` differs (esp. reading the 2-band `IGasReadModel`, a float dose, a non-deterministic/host-only dose, or a private-near-bubble read instead of the authoritative field) is a self-authored substitution needing owner-ack — otherwise STOP-escalate.

## the determinism crux (read twice)

Sc-sense adds the FIRST non-gas replicated state (actor position + dose). The traps: (a) reading the doomed 2-band `IGasReadModel` (dependency S4 deletes) instead of the near authoritative field; (b) a float dose or a host-only/non-deterministic dose (two peers diverge under a green gas checksum → the «чексумм ЗЕЛЁНЫЙ, физика мусор» desync one level up); (c) folding the actor position/dose non-canonically (actor-keyed order matters); (d) treating the actor position as a same-tick read of live gas instead of the COMMITTED revision; (e) mutating the gas `MeaningChecksum` to carry player state when SPEC asked for a separate player-kinematics hash. The PRIMARY mechanical falsifiers:
- **DOSE-IS-DETERMINISTIC RED:** two loopback peers with the SAME lockstep actor-position stream over a multi-type gas scene compute a BYTE-IDENTICAL per-actor dose; a planted float / host-only / same-tick-read realization MUST FAIL.
- **AUTHORITATIVE-NOT-2BAND RED:** the exposure read resolves against the near authoritative field; a planted read of `IGasReadModel`/`CoarseGasReadModel` is caught (the near read gives the per-TypeId answer the 2-band cannot).
- **NO-REGRESSION:** a no-actor scene reproduces the committed gas golden byte-identically; the separate player digest is SKIP-ZERO/additive and empty when no actor participates.
- **PLAYER-KINEMATICS/DOSE-HASH RED:** two peers with DIVERGENT actor positions OR divergent accumulated dose desync loudly in the player digest (the §4 seam 7 digest catches actor divergence the gas hash does not).
