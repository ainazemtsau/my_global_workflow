- **W5 — Strength and body-composition contour**
  - status: answered
  - requirement: The strength contour maintains a reproducible non-maximal baseline, progressive or regressive rules, technique constraints, recovery checks, and a response to sustained strength or wellbeing decline during weight loss. →S1/S2
  - acceptance: Review can distinguish hold, progress, regress, substitute, deload, rebuild, and cross-domain review needs without relying on a maximal test or chat memory. →S2/W9

- **W6 — Activity and conditioning contour**
  - status: answered

  - acceptance: The system can issue a feasible activity option for the current context and can downshift or stop it without treating competition, streaks, or scores as authority. →S2/G-TA-8

- **W7 — Mechanical safety brake**
  - status: answered
