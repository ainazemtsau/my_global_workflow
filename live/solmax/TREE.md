root:
  id: g-zara
  status: shaped
  goal: |
    Zaratusta — a personal, deeply personalized AI exocortex the owner

    All hold unless the charter is explicitly revised:

    This is the enduring Zaratusta outcome: a real personal exocortex,

  children:
    - id: g-zara-operate
      status: parked
      goal: |
        Zaratusta is one coherent Personal Operating System for the owner.

        It helps preserve focus, productivity and objectivity; does not
        lose commitments, ideas or personal context; and operates goals,
        long initiatives, working periods, weeks, days, tasks, journals,
        processes and reviews.

        It behaves as a strict personal manager rather than a passive
        archive or generic prompt: it maintains current work state,
        supplies the relevant next action, challenges weak reasoning,
        protects accepted priorities and preserves the owner's explicit
        override.

      done_when: |
        All hold:

        1. A coherent work-entity model exists and covers at least:
           directions;
           goals;
           multi-week projects, programs or courses that need not become
           directions;
           tasks and commitments;
           backlog and ideas without automatic commitment;
           recurring processes and rituals;
           journals;
           updateable personal facts and preferences.

           Exact meanings and distinctions between these entities are
           explicitly defined rather than silently assumed.

        2. A coherent operating loop links:
           long or medium horizon;
           current period;
           week;
           day;
           concrete tasks/actions;
           actual progress;
           review;
           subsequent plan/state changes.

           A 12-week cycle, month or another planning model is considered
           through research and owner choice rather than hard-coded.

        3. Every load-bearing operation exists as an explicit process
           contract rather than a sentence inside one monolithic prompt.

           At minimum the system has separately inspectable contracts for:
           intake and personal-context updating;
           horizon planning;
           week planning;
           day startup and operation;
           task tracking;
           multi-week initiative operation;
           free logging;
           on-plan response;
           off-plan response;
           remaining-work recomposition;
           day review;
           week/period review;
           backlog/capture;
           journals and rituals;
           unknown/research routing;
           process mutation;
           durable-state writing;
           continuation in a new chat.

           Each contract defines input, required context, relevant states,
           output, state change, unknowns and authority boundaries.

        4. Logging is interactive operation, not passive storage.

           After an owner update, the manager chooses a relevant bounded
           response such as:
           acknowledge continued execution;
           show only the nearest action;
           identify a deviation;
           propose a correction;
           recompose the remaining day;
           route a side question;
           preserve an observation for review.

           The response policy is researched and explicitly specified.
           Adaptability may not substitute for defined behavior. Default
           responses do not overload the owner with unrelated state.

        5. A multi-week initiative preserves its own outcome, state, log,
           nearest actions, link to the active horizon and stop/completion
           condition without being forced into a separate direction.

        6. Context is scoped by operation. A day, week, task, journal or
           research chat receives the necessary state slice plus common
           mandatory rules, not the whole personal archive.

        7. Research is a system route.

           When an operation encounters an unknown, dead end, missing
           evidence or improvement request, it:
           states the unknown;
           chooses between an owner question and bounded research;
           produces a self-contained child research request;
           receives a structured result;
           only then proposes a process or decision change.

           Hidden assumptions are prohibited.

        8. Durable-state changes use one writer contract across supported
           surfaces. A work session returns structured changes and a
           writer applies them, whether invocation is manual or automated.

        9. The system does not collapse into a single large instruction.
           The coordinating manager, specialized process contracts, state
           schemas and routing rules are separate versioned artifacts
           that can be checked and changed independently while remaining
           coherent.

        10. The complete minimum operates as one compatible system.
            Isolated week, day, memory, task or review specifications do
            not close this node without compatible entities, state,
            routing and writer behavior.

      why: |
        This is the first real Zaratusta subsystem and directly supplies
        deep personal operation beyond a plain disconnected chat.

      detail: history/2026-06-25-s-zara-remap-001.md

    - id: g-zara-substrate
      status: parked
      goal: |
        Zaratusta has a small, stable and extensible capability substrate
        extracted from mechanisms actually required by the Personal
        Operating System.

        New processes, memory, tools, channels and views connect through
        explicit contracts without repeatedly rewriting the whole system.
        The substrate serves used capabilities rather than becoming an
        independent architecture project.

      done_when: |
        All hold:

        1. Repeated mechanisms observed in real operating processes have
           yielded common contracts for at least:
           specialized-process launch;
           scoped-context transfer;
           durable-state read/write;
           child research/discussion;
           structured result return;
           owner approval;
           writer application;
           interruption recovery;
           capability registration.

        2. Domain logic, common process mechanics and execution surfaces
           are explicitly separated.

        3. A new capability can connect without changing already working
           domain processes except at a named extension point.

        4. A capability contract identifies:
           id and purpose;
           type;
           input;
           required context;
           output;
           readable and writable state;
           effects;
           approval requirements;
           failure/return behavior;
           contract version.

        5. A common record of significant transitions supports:
           continuation;
           explanation of current state;
           competing-change detection;
           rollback or restoration.

        6. Extensibility is exercised by adding at least one new real
           capability demanded after the first operating-system contour,
           through existing seams without redesigning the substrate.

        7. Every former RLK element — registry, ledger, envelope,
           effect gate and Engine/Store seams — receives an independent
           keep/change/defer/drop verdict based on demonstrated need.

        8. The substrate contains no domain-specific rules for weeks,
           tasks, journals, motivation or personal entities.

      why: |
        It carries proven extensibility while preventing another
        architecture-first perpetual-core route.

      detail: history/2026-06-25-s-zara-remap-001.md

        All hold:

        1. Zaratusta is used across at least 3 distinct areas of the

        2. Each area has at least one working capability or process with
           explicit purpose, input/output, context, state, launch rules,
           completion/review criteria and authority boundaries.

        3. At least one hard process depends on accumulated personal
           context, multiple stages, specialized sources or tools,

        4. Multiple capability classes exist where demanded by use,

        5. Every new capability follows a lifecycle:
           need detected;
           existing process checked first;
           research when needed;
           outcome and boundaries defined;
           connection to other processes defined;
           implementation through the substrate;
           use on real work;
           hold/change/simplify/drop by evidence.

        6. Capabilities do not become disconnected applications.
           Significant commitments return to the coordinating operating
           system; source-of-truth boundaries remain explicit; context is
           not copied without control; no parallel planning system forms.

        7. Stored information has a named purpose, reader, update rule,
           staleness rule, correction path and cross-domain boundary.

        8. New capabilities do not require continual redesign of the
           substrate or operating manager. Recurrent contract failure
           triggers explicit substrate review rather than hidden
           exceptions.

        9. Former EXOCORTEX ideas remain candidates, not an automatic
           roadmap. No capability gains priority merely because it
           appeared in an earlier concept.

      why: |
        It completes the broader Zaratusta mission after the personal
        operating foundation and reusable substrate exist.
