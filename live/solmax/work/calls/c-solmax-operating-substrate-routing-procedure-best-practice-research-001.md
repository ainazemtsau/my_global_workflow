  Q7 plain question:
  "Как process routes intake to procedures, and what is reusable
  procedure structure vs local play/process?"

  Why it matters:
  Routing and procedure semantics are where a durable AI-led process
  becomes more than generic conversation. New process packs should not
  reinvent procedure invocation, uncertainty, bounded delegation,
  ownership, failure and return semantics, while the substrate must not
  impose one domain's procedure catalogue or current router technology.

  Accepted parent locks:
  - Q1-Q6 remain unchanged.
  - Operating-substrate is a stable model- and carrier-neutral kernel
    with pluggable process packs.
  - Service zones are semantic responsibility families, not physical
    components.
  - Atomic claims have one primary normative owner.
  - Procedures are first-class process definitions rather than informal
    prompt suggestions, but concrete procedure catalogues and domain
    routing rules are process-pack concerns.
  - Invoke, delegate and handoff have different ownership effects.
  - Query, event, evidence, propose, validate, authorize, apply, result
    and continue remain distinct.
  - Every open obligation has one current next-action owner.
  - Delegation retains parent integration and accountability.
  - Capability does not imply authority.
  - Process-pack instantiation uses staged admission and activation.
  - Owner context is admitted and projected under bounded purpose.
  - Accepted state, history, source, evidence, checkpoint and memory
    remain distinct.
  - Routing must use accepted state and relevant sources without treating
    memory, transcript or newest event as authoritative truth.
  - Procedure or routing definition changes do not silently reinterpret
    active work.
  - No final service-block list, procedure schema, carrier, provider,
    runtime, router algorithm or implementation is accepted.

  Research pressure includes:
  - procedure definition versus invocation versus execution attempt;
  - procedure identity and compatibility across versions;
  - deterministic workflow routing versus model-directed routing;
  - applicability, preconditions, required context and capability;
  - uncertainty, ambiguity, missing-input and escalation behavior;
  - procedure selection versus ownership transfer;
  - nested procedures, delegation, cancellation and return;
  - local process-pack procedure policy versus reusable semantics;
  - degraded behavior when a procedure or capability is unavailable;
  - recovery and continuation when routing or execution is interrupted;
  - owner steering without routing every uncertainty to the owner;
  - anti-bureaucracy and live-use pressure.

  Current Direction OS plays, Zaratusta behavior and researched
  frameworks are evidence only, not substrate authority.
