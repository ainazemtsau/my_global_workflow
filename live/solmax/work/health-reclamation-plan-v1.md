# Health Reclamation plan v1

Status: **APPROVED - frozen for fresh BUILD (owner: "Approve plan v1", 2026-07-13).**

This is a plan for a future process-pack candidate. It is not a process
candidate, an admitted definition, an activation, a training prescription, a
nutrition prescription, or evidence of useful live behavior.

## The intended outcome

Create one new owner-facing process named **Health Reclamation**
("Возвращение формы и потенциала"). Its purpose is to support one person's
safe, sustainable return to physical activity and gym training, body-change
work, food and recovery habits, and a voluntary habit-support line through
meaningful action and review.

It begins from the owner's current intent, not from a menu, a copied workout,
or a generic health template.

## What the owner will experience

The process will be an ordinary conversation, not a form or a dashboard:

1. It helps name the reason for the change and the next honest step.
2. It creates a short first cycle only after the private safety route permits
   a concrete physical or nutrition intervention.
3. It supports a daily/session choice without converting a missed action into
   debt or punishment.
4. It offers a periodic review that changes only the part that created friction.
5. It can use an owner-chosen short focus ritual, stronger warrior-like
   language, music, or a creative text when helpful. All of these are optional
   and removable.

The normal result is one useful next step, one material question, a clear
safety block with a human/professional owner, or a portable continuation.

## Fixed boundaries

The build must:

- create a fresh process; never import, reactivate, or silently reuse previous
  Health menus, recipes, workouts, raw logs, schedules, or body metrics;
- keep health measurements, diagnoses, medications, private symptoms, and
  clinician material outside the repository and Direction OS;
- distinguish owner goals and private inputs from accepted state, evidence,
  history, and a future effective process definition;
- treat a ritual or persona as voluntary support language, never religion,
  pressure, diagnosis, or authority;
- treat a missed session, food friction, or an owner-identified habit lapse as
  information for a small recovery step, never grounds for shame, fasting, or
  compensatory overtraining;
- route uncertainty or safety concerns to the correct qualified human rather
  than pretending to clear an exercise or nutrition intervention;
- require an independent validation before admission, and a separate
  authorization plus authoritative apply outcome before activation;
- keep structural admissibility, useful live behavior, and demonstrated reuse
  as three independent verdicts.

The build must not create automatic surveillance, routine polling, a medical
service, an external-effect agent, a universal health template, or a claim that
the plan itself already works.

## Planned candidate shape

The future pack will use a small, process-specific set of Markdown artifacts:

- a plain-language owner brief;
- a contract for objective, applicability, boundary, and owner-facing closure;
- a policy for privacy, source/evidence distinctions, authority, safety,
  capabilities, degraded routes, lifecycle, and correction;
- a local routing catalogue;
- concrete procedures for framing, private readiness, forming a first cycle,
  supporting a real day/session, review/recovery, escalation, lifecycle, and
  fresh resume;
- default-FAIL coverage and adverse scenarios, including no legacy import,
  safety uncertainty, lapse recovery, optional ritual removal, missing
  capability, no silent external effect, correction, and fresh resume;
- a privacy-safe portable continuation.

The representation is a bounded implementation choice for this process, not a
universal manifest or schema for future processes.

## Evidence and review plan

1. The builder self-checks every declared obligation, file reference, trailer,
   no-import boundary, privacy boundary, safety route, and no-activation claim.
2. Existing repository gates run before deliver; their green result is only
   wiring/hygiene evidence, never semantic admission.
3. A fresh independent reviewer attempts to refute the completed candidate,
   particularly for invented health input, medical-authority leakage,
   coercive/forced ritual, diary-only behavior, legacy import, punitive lapse
   behavior, privacy leakage, silent fallback, and false activation.
4. Only after a valid structural/admission result may a separate activation
   proposal ask for the minimum private readiness and authority decisions.
5. A later real owner run, not artifacts, determines useful-live behavior.

## Real cuts for this first build

- no old menu or training-program migration;
- no exact workout, calorie target, meal plan, body metric, or medical advice
  baked into the candidate;
- no wearable, gym-app, dashboard, scheduler, calendar integration, or
  background monitoring;
- no automated music generation or required ritual;
- no external purchases, messages, bookings, or other effects;
- no claim of recurring reliability, usefulness, or cross-process reuse.
