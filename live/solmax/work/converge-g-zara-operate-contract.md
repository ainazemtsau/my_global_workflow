W2 — What may the manager decide?

  refusal/escalation, which allowed route applies, and whether work stays
  inside Zaratusta workspace or needs owner approval/integration. It may

W6 — What may the manager refuse?
status: answered

W8 — What are the effect tiers?
status: answered
answer: Effect tiers are:
  ET0 talk-only: answer, critique, ask, explain, classify, draft, propose,
  compare, summarize source context or refuse/route with no durable state
  change except the conversation itself.
  ET1 reversible internal Zaratusta workspace operation: capture, backlog
  entry, source registry entry, process draft, context summary, owner-context
  note, example/test, research request or reversible state change inside
  Zaratusta when auditable and within an accepted process.
  ET2 owner-approval/integration-required: accepted plan/commitment change,
  accepted process-rule change, external action, irreversible action,
  deletion, spend, message/send, source-system route that creates work for
  another system, write outside Zaratusta through a narrow integration, or
  any action with material consequence beyond internal reversible Zaratusta
  state.
  ET3 blocked-until-routed: Direction OS writes; other repo/direction/project
  writes without explicit narrow integration; silent spend; irreversible or
  external action without explicit approval; disabling owner override; hidden
  self-rewrite; claiming source-system truth without source context; presenting
  source-owned/high-stakes instructions as manager-authored authority rather
  than source-backed/owner-approved process output. ET3 is not a domain list:
  topic work remains allowed through source/context/process routing, but the
  blocked effect itself is not performed. →GLOSSARY:G3[tiered]
  →GLOSSARY:G7[explicit] →GLOSSARY:G8[inviolable]
  →GLOSSARY:G9[read-not-own] →GLOSSARY:G10[topic-open]
  →GLOSSARY:G10[safety-routed] →S1 →S6 →S7
acceptance: implementation must force every state-changing, routing or
  source-owning output to carry an effect tier; ET2 requires explicit owner
  approval or accepted integration before effect; ET3 is refused or routed
  without performing the blocked effect. →GLOSSARY:G3[auditable]
