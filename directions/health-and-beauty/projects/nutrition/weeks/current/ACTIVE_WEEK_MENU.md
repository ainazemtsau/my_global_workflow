## Prep Plan

### Step 0: Eat Breakfast Before Batch Prep
After shopping, make Tuna Feta Breakfast Omelet Roll.
Do not start batch prep hungry.

### Step 1: Cook Rice In Instant Pro
- 500-600 g dry rice
- water according to rice type
- salt
Pressure Cook High 4 minutes, Natural Release 10 minutes.
Cool and portion separately.

### Step 2: Marinate Chicken Shawarma
Mix yogurt, lemon, garlic, oil, spices, salt.
Coat chicken.
Minimum marinade: 30 minutes.
Better: 2-4 hours if available.

### Step 3: Cook Turkey Potato Chili In Instant Pro
Use Sauté for aromatics and turkey.
Deglaze well.
Pressure Cook High 6 minutes because potato replaces sweet potato.
Natural Release 7 minutes.
Finish with lemon/lime and yogurt at serving.

### Step 4: Cook Chicken Shawarma In Ninja
Air Fry 200°C for 15-20 minutes, turning halfway.
Cook in batches; do not crowd.

### Step 5: Cook Potato Wedges In Ninja
Air Fry 190-200°C for 22-30 minutes.
Shake halfway.
Portion separately.

### Step 6: Cook Greek Turkey Feta Meatballs In Ninja
Air Fry 200°C for 8 minutes, turn, then 3-5 minutes more.
Cool and portion.

### Step 7: Build Breakfast Kits
Prepare containers:
- chicken shawarma 150 g + cheese/feta 30-40 g
- turkey meatballs 3-5 pieces
- potato wedges 150-250 g
- tuna cans left unopened
Eggs are added fresh in the morning.

### Step 8: Greek Salad Constructor
Store separately:
- cucumbers/pepper
- tomatoes, preferably cut day-of
- feta
- olives
- dressing components
Do not pre-mix all salad for 4 days.

### Step 9: Vacuum / Storage
Cool food before sealing.
Fridge:
- Thursday-Friday portions
Freezer or vacuum + freezer:
- Sunday chili portion
- extra meatballs
- extra chicken shawarma
Do not vacuum hot food.
Do not rely on vacuum sealing to override normal refrigeration rules.
