# Research Source Register

This register controls external research used by Workflow Governance.

## Source-quality rules

- Prefer official docs, primary papers, reputable reports.
- Separate source-backed facts from inference.
- Record date accessed.
- Do not change workflow based on one weak source.
- Do not treat blog posts, social posts, or unsourced claims as sufficient authority for workflow changes.

## Required source record

```yaml
source:
  source_id:
  title:
  url_or_path:
  publisher_or_author:
  source_type:
  date_accessed:
  source_quality:
  key_source_backed_facts:
  inference_or_implication:
  workflow_change_recommended:
  confidence:
```
